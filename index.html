about Kurdistan

Kurdistan, broadly defined geographic region traditionally inhabited mainly by Kurds.

Population. The number of Kurds living in Southwest Asia is estimated at between 30 and 45 million, with another one or two million living in the Kurdish diaspora. Kurds comprise anywhere from 18 to 25% of the population in Turkey, 15 to 20% in Iraq; 10% in Iran; and 9% in Syria.
